How Carbo works

Simple process to become world citizens. 

1- Footprint Calculation

There is 2 main way to emit CO2; Digital usage and Real world consumption. We gather big data that help us to estimate the most accurate results of your emission in both areas. 

2- Offset Estimation

The price per kg of carbon comes from the advanced machine learning models that constantly analyze different prices from the most reliable data sources. Our model comes out the final price for your offset, depending on your total emission.

3- Carbon NFT Certification

You may mint your Carbon NFT through several plugins such as Metamask, Trustwallet, etc. You will receive your NFT in your wallet after the transaction is done.

What does Carbo do?

Our voluntary marketplace, based on blockchain technology, will enable carbon removal suppliers to connect directly with buyers, improve efficiency, and reduce costs.

Where does my money go to?

100% of each kg you pay for goes directly to supporting our suppliers, while Carbo collects a 20% transaction fee to help keep the marketplace running. This not only rewards suppliers for implementing sustainable practices into their workflow, but provides further incentives for sustainable organizations to provide even more carbon removals in the future.
