Governance

XPN is the utility and the governance token for the Xapiens platform. Holders will vote on the whitelisting of new borrowers to earn additional XPN through an incentive reward scheme. Eventually, a full system of decentralized governance will be introduced, allowing XPN holders to propose, vote and implement future upgrades to the platform.

Staking&Yield Farming

Pre-Staking will be available after the Pre-Sales. XPN staking will be required action for borrowers, who must stake an amount of XPN to access an area of the protocol where they can make a proposal. Additionally, liquidity providers earn additional XPN rewards, enhancing pool interest rates to attractive levels.

Community Support

- Liquidity locked before the Presale
- Audit&KYC verification
- Strong marketing to support continuous growth with influencer campaigns.
